What Is Tarot Reading?

Tarot reading is an ancient practice that uses a deck of 78 symbolic cards to gain insight into the past, present, and future. Our certified tarot readers connect with your energy to interpret the cards drawn and provide meaningful guidance tailored to your unique situation.

Whether you're seeking clarity on relationships, career decisions, spiritual growth, or life purpose, a tarot reading can illuminate the path ahead and empower you to make informed decisions with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tarot reading service.

Our online tarot readings take place via secure video call. Your reader will connect with your energy remotely, shuffle and draw cards, and provide a full interpretation just as they would in a face-to-face session. Many clients find online readings equally powerful and more convenient.
No special preparation is needed. We recommend finding a quiet, comfortable space and thinking about the questions or areas of life you'd like guidance on. Come with an open mind and heart — the cards respond best to genuine curiosity and willingness to receive insight.
Absolutely! All our Full Reading and Deep Dive sessions are automatically recorded and a copy is sent to your email within 24 hours. For Quick Insight sessions, recording is available as an add-on for $5. We encourage revisiting your reading as new insights often emerge over time.
You can ask about virtually any area of life — relationships, career, finances, health, spiritual growth, major decisions, and more. Open-ended questions tend to yield the richest readings. Instead of "Will I get the job?", try "What do I need to know about my career path?" for deeper insights.
